About Me

I’ve been practicing yoga and Pilates for over 10 years, since 2013. Over time, this practice has supported me both physically and mentally, and it’s something I’m passionate about sharing with others.

In my earlier years, I was focused on achieving the “perfect” pose. As my practice evolved—especially into my late 30s—my approach shifted towards slower, more mindful movement that prioritises strength, balance, and restoration.

My teaching now reflects this: a calm, supportive space where you can build strength gently, move with intention, and allow time for deep rest.

Practice with me

Vinyasa Flow

Slow, guided sequences that build strength and mobility while connecting movement to breath.

Strength & Stability

Focused sessions to improve balance, control, and joint stability using controlled, low-impact movement.

Yoga Nidra

Guided relaxation practices designed to calm the nervous system and support deep mental and physical rest.

My classes are designed to help you build strength in a supportive way, while also creating space to slow down, reset, and restore.
