About Me

Move with intention. Build strength gently. Make space to rest.

I’ve been practicing yoga and Pilates for over 10 years, since 2013. Over time, this practice has supported me both physically and mentally, and it’s something I’m passionate about sharing with others.

My approach has evolved from striving for “perfect” poses to a slower, more intuitive practice that prioritises strength, balance, and restoration.

My teaching offers a calm, supportive space where you can move with intention, build strength gently, and create space to rest.
