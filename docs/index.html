About Me

Move with awareness. Gently build strength. Make space for rest.

I’ve been practicing yoga for over 12 years, and it has become the foundation of my physical and mental wellbeing.

Over time, my practice has evolved from dynamic movement toward a slower, more grounding and nervous system regulating approach.

My classes offer a calm, supportive space to move with awareness, gently build strength, and create space for rest within the demands of everyday life — allowing strength, mobility, and ease to develop naturally over time.

Practice with me

Vinyasa Flow

Slow, guided sequences that build strength and mobility while connecting movement to breath.

Strength & Stability

Focused sessions to improve balance, control, and joint stability using controlled, low-impact movement.

Yoga Nidra

Guided relaxation practices designed to calm the nervous system and support deep mental and physical rest.

My classes are designed to help you build strength in a supportive way, combining gentle stretching, mindful movement, and rest to help you slow down, reset, and restore.
